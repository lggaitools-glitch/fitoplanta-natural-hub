# FitoPlantaMed — Video Presentation Strategy

## Brand Summary (for video context)

**FitoPlantaMed** is a Brazilian Portuguese-language platform about evidence-based phytotherapy (herbal medicine), medicinal plants, and natural supplements.

**Marta Leni** is the face and authority of the brand:
- Farmacêutica Clínica (Clinical Pharmacist) — CRF-SP 93119
- Graduated from Universidade Anhanguera (Pharmacy)
- Mestrado (Master's) in Biotecnologia from UFSCar
- Specialized in Clinical Phytotherapy & Prescription of Phytotherapeutics
- 20+ years of volunteer health work
- Background in compounding pharmacy (magistral formulations)
- Taught and lectured at institutions like FIOCRUZ, IBPM, Racine
- Areas: phytotherapy, medicinal plants, integrative practices (PICS), pharmacognosy

**Affiliate partner:** BioVittare (natural supplements)

## Video Concept: "Quem é o FitoPlantaMed?"

### Option A: Professional Brand Intro (60-90 seconds)

**Purpose:** Website landing page, social ads, YouTube channel trailer, investor/partner pitch.

**Structure:**

1. **Hook (0-5s):** Text overlay or voiceover — *"Você confia no que lê sobre plantas medicinais?"*
2. **Problem (5-15s):** Quick montage of misinformation — miracle cure claims, random blogs, unverified supplements. Voiceover: *"Na internet, qualquer um fala sobre fitoterapia. Mas quem realmente entende?"*
3. **Introduction (15-30s):** Marta Leni appears. Name + credentials on screen. *"Eu sou Marta Leni, Farmacêutica Clínica com mais de 20 anos de experiência em fitoterapia baseada em evidências."*
4. **Mission (30-50s):** What FitoPlantaMed does — *"Criamos conteúdo sério, acessível e baseado em ciência sobre plantas medicinais, fitoterápicos e saúde natural."* B-roll of the website, articles, plants, lab imagery.
5. **Trust signals (50-65s):** Quick flashes of credentials — CRF-SP, UFSCar, FIOCRUZ, 20+ years, volunteer work.
6. **CTA (65-80s):** *"Visite fitoplantamed.com e descubra a fitoterapia de verdade."* Logo + URL.

### Option B: Personal Story Format (2-3 minutes)

**Purpose:** YouTube "About" video, social media storytelling, email welcome sequence.

**Structure:**

1. **Cold open:** Marta in a natural setting (garden, herbs). Genuine, warm tone.
2. **Origin story:** Why she got into phytotherapy. The gap she saw between science and public misinformation.
3. **Credentials journey:** Pharmacy degree → biochemist → phytotherapy specialization → MSc Biotecnologia → 20 years of practice and teaching.
4. **Volunteer angle:** This is gold for trust. 20+ years of free health work = genuine mission, not just commerce.
5. **The "why" of FitoPlantaMed:** Bridge between academic knowledge and everyday people. Making evidence-based info accessible.
6. **Transparency moment:** "Yes, we recommend products. But we only recommend what we'd use ourselves. Here's how we choose." (Links to /transparencia)
7. **CTA:** Subscribe, explore the site, sign up for the newsletter.

### Option C: AI-Generated / No-Camera Version

If Marta isn't available for filming (or as a fast MVP):

**Tools to produce without a camera:**
- **Voiceover:** ElevenLabs or similar TTS in Brazilian Portuguese (female, warm, professional voice). Or hire a VO artist on Fiverr ($20-50).
- **Visuals:** Stock footage (herbs, labs, nature) + screen recordings of the website + animated text overlays.
- **Editing:** CapCut (free), DaVinci Resolve, or Canva video.
- **AI avatar option:** HeyGen or Synthesia — create a professional presenter. However, this could undermine trust if people realize it's AI (risky for a health brand). Better to use voiceover + B-roll.

**Recommended MVP approach:**
1. Write the script in Portuguese
2. Record voiceover (real person preferred, ElevenLabs as backup)
3. Combine with stock footage + website screenshots + animated credentials
4. Add subtitles (most social video is watched muted)
5. Export in multiple formats: 16:9 (YouTube), 9:16 (Reels/TikTok/Shorts), 1:1 (feed posts)

## Script Direction Notes

**Tone:** Warm but authoritative. Not clinical/cold. Not salesy. Think "trusted expert who genuinely cares."

**Language:** Brazilian Portuguese, accessible. Avoid jargon — or explain it when used. Marta should feel like the smart friend who happens to be a pharmacist.

**Visual palette:** Greens, golds, earth tones (matching the website's design language).

**Music:** Soft, organic, instrumental. Something that says "nature meets science" — acoustic guitar or soft piano, not corporate stock music.
